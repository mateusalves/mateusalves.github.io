About Me

Hello! My name is Mateus Alves and I'm fascinated to use engineering to build things that can improve people's lives


I am currently an Electronic Engineer and I work as a Embedded Systems Developer Engineer at OnBoard Mobility. I am responsible, among other things, to develop embedded systems solutions in the Internet of Things context. I have experience in PCB design, assembly, testing and programming of MCUs.

I received my Bachelor's degrees in Electronics Engineering from University of Brasilia - UnB with a period of exchange program at Wayne State University and University of California, Los Angeles During college I had experience in research projects related to the field of Biomedical Engineering developing electronic circuits and their programming codes. In the exchange program, in addition to the subjects studied, I participated in other research projects, ranging from programming robotic arms to improvements in smartphone applications.

When I'm not involved in the engineering world, I have diverse interests such as the stock market , music , series / movies / anime , personal development books and the most amazing game ever made: Rocket League .

Welcome to my website and feel free to contact me - even if it's just to buy me a coffee.
